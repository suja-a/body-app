I am Aditi and this is what I believe in.

"You are not your body. You are a complex and beautiful being, worthy of love and respect, regardless of your appearance." - Anonymous



All my life, I was picked on for being small. When people do that, I just ignore it and move on with a smile. Body shaming is a form of abuse, and it can have long lasting negative consequences for children and adults alike. It can damage their self-esteem, body image, and mental health.

I wanted to create a space for all to feel body-positive. This is a space for others like me, to share your stories.

Disclaimer: All bodies are beautiful. This app does not endorse any particular diet or exercise plan, and it is not intended to be a substitute for professional medical advice. If you are struggling with body image issues, please know that you are not alone. There are many resources available to help you, including professional therapists and support groups. Please reach out for help if you need it. Be critical of the content you see. Not all body positivity content is created equal. Some content may be harmful or unrealistic. Be mindful of your own triggers. If certain types of content are triggering for you, avoid it. Don't compare yourself to others. Everyone's body is different, and that's okay. Focus on the things you love about yourself. Make a list of all the things you appreciate about your body, both physical and non-physical. Be patient. It takes time to learn to love and accept your body.
